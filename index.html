At South Stand, it's always happy hour...

Back in 2015, four friends noticed a lack of bespoke drinking experiences in our beloved town. We wanted a bar where you could get sensational locally brewed craft beer, and we wanted to drink in a place where we felt welcome, and where people knew us by name. We wanted somewhere where we could make memories, so we built it, and we hope you enjoy it...
